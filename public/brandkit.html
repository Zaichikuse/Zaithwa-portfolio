06 Usage Guidelines
Always Do
  • Use Deep Magenta as the primary brand color in all materials
  • Pair Playfair Display headlines with DM Sans body text always
  • Keep "Her" in italic whenever possible — it's the brand's heartbeat
  • Use gold as a celebratory accent for achievements and milestones
  • Maintain generous white space — the brand breathes, it doesn't crowd
  • Always lead with warmth and empowerment in tone and visual choices
Never Do
  • Never use the dark theme — TechHer MW is light, warm, and uplifting
  • Never stretch or distort the logo mark — always keep proportions locked
  • Never use harsh neons or cold blues — they conflict with the brand warmth
  • Never place the logo on a busy background without a clean overlay
  • Never use more than 2 typefaces in any single design piece
  • Never write in a tone that is exclusive, cold, or corporate — always human
